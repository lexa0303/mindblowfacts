Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

1. Introduction

MindBlow Facts ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our Service.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect a social media account (e.g., TikTok, YouTube), we access only the permissions you explicitly grant:

  • Basic profile information (username, display name) to identify the connected account.
  • Video publishing permissions to upload content on your behalf.

We do not collect personal data beyond what is necessary for the Service to function.

3. How We Use Information

Information collected is used solely to:

  • Authenticate and maintain your connection to social media platforms.
  • Publish video content to your authorized accounts.

4. Data Storage

Authentication tokens are stored locally on the device running the Service. We do not transmit or store your credentials on external servers.

5. Third-Party Services

The Service interacts with third-party platforms (YouTube, TikTok). Your use of those platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Retention

We retain authentication tokens only as long as needed for the Service to function. You may revoke access at any time through your social media account settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our social media channels.
